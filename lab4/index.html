Challenges

My biggest challenge was detailing out the intructions without being too indepth or too vague. I also wanted to use terms (like If and Then) that could translated into JavaScript easier

Results

Pseudocode for How to Cook Pasta:
  1. Get a pot of water
  2. Boil water on medium to high temperature
  3. Add Sea salt to the water
  4. Add box of penne pasta
  5. Set a timer for 10 minutes
  6. While the pasta waits on the 10 minute timer, Add oil, garlic, and diced onion into a saute pan
  7. Once the garlic and onion begin to brown, pour a can of crushed tomatoes and tomato sauce into pan.
  8. If sauce is boiling, then add half a can of tomato paste
  9. Add a pinch of salt, italian spice, oregeno, and sugar, then stir and let it sit for two minutes
  10. Stir, test, and strain pasta at the 10 minute mark, if pasta is al dente
  11. Mix strained penne pasta into saute pan with sauce
  12. Top off the pan with some parmesean and enjoy!
How to Create a Hangman Game:
  1. Before the game begins have one player choose a random word in their head.
  2. Enter users word input
  3. Draw a game board using two vertical and two horizontal lines.
    (The board should look like an upsidedown capital T with a foot)
  4. Player 2 guesses the letters that build Player 1's word.
  5. If Player 2 guesses letter correct, then show correct letter
  6. If Player 2 guesses letter wrong, then hide wrong letter and add body part to hangman
  7. If hangman gets all body parts before the word is complete, Player 1 wins
  8. If word is complete before hangman gets all body parts, Player 2 wins
